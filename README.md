Customer Type

Type of customer (e.g., Member, Normal)

Gender

Customer's gender

Product Line

Category of product purchased

Unit Price

Price per unit of the product

Quantity

Number of items purchased

Tax 5%

Total

Total cost including tax

Date

Date of purchase

Time

Time of purchase

Payment Method

COGS

Cost of goods sold

Gross Margin Percentage

Profit margin percentage

Gross Income
